About

Hi, I'm Eric Berry.

I started Berry Bookkeeping because I kept seeing the same thing — small business owners making important decisions with books that were months behind, inaccurate, or impossible to understand.

My goal is simple: give you clean numbers, clear answers, and the confidence to grow your business. I work with growing Edmonton businesses that have outgrown owner-managed books and need accurate, reliable financials without hiring in-house.

With a Bachelor of Commerce degree from MacEwan University and a background in finance and accounting across multiple industries, I bring more than bookkeeping to the table — I bring context.
